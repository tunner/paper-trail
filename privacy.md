Privacy Policy for PaperTrail

Effective Date: 2025 01 01

PaperTrail does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
All checklists, templates, and workflow progress are stored locally on your device. No information leaves your device at any time.

---------

Information We Collect

PaperTrail does not collect any personal information.
The app does not require a user account and does not communicate with any server.

---------

Internet Use

PaperTrail works fully offline.
The app does not require an internet connection for its features and does not send data across the internet.

---------

Third Party Services

PaperTrail does not use analytics, tracking tools, advertising SDKs, or any external services that process user data.

---------

User Generated Data

Any checklists you create remain stored only on your device.
Deleting the app will remove all locally stored checklists and progress.

---------

Children

The app is suitable for all ages. Since PaperTrail collects no data at all, it complies with child privacy regulations by design.

---------

Changes to this Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page.
