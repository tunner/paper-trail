PaperTrail Support

Welcome to the PaperTrail support page.
PaperTrail helps you navigate bureaucracy with clear, step by step checklists for real administrative tasks. This page provides basic support information, answers common questions, and explains the app’s privacy approach.

---------

What is PaperTrail

PaperTrail is a simple offline app that provides:
	•	High quality workflows for common government procedures
	•	Step by step checklists to avoid missing documents
	•	The ability to create your own personal checklists
	•	Progress tracking to help you complete complicated tasks
	•	A fully offline experience with no account required

PaperTrail is designed for real day to day bureaucracy. Whether you are applying for a passport, registering an address, or preparing official forms, the app gives you a clear sequence of steps.

---------

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app work offline?

Yes. All workflows and checklists work completely offline.

Does the app store my data online?

No. All your checklists and progress are stored locally on your device only.

Do you collect analytics?

No. PaperTrail does not use analytics, tracking SDKs, or any third party tools.

Can I suggest new workflows?

Yes. You can send suggestions or feedback using the contact information below.
